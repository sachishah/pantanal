A POS built for Customer Retention

Pantanal offers simplicity for Brazilian businesses. We transform and aggregate your company's sales and customer data so you can focus on generating insights that matter. Stay in the know, spot trends as they happen, and push your business further.

Our Platform

All-in-One Platform

Pantanal integrates different data sources into a single pipeline, allowing you to get a birds-eye view of your business. We overlay your sales data with customer data, using your existing loyalty programs and third-party data sources around tax IDs and census bureau information.

Cloud-Based Architecture

With Pantanal, you have remote access to real-time data streams and reports. Monitor your business, anytime, anywhere. We have end-to-end data encryption at rest and in transit, so you can be assured that your data is secure.

Hadoop-based analytical tools

Pantanal allows you to run analyses on your data, no matter how large or complex. Our software is engineered to allow business owners to gain quick actionable insights over large datasets fast.

Improved Customer Management

Pantanal allows you to know your customer to make targeted sales decisions. We focused on reducing customer churn and maximizing their lifetime value, by allowing you to proactively manage your customer relationships.
